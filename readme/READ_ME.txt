HOW TO USE DEVICE:

STEP 1 PLUG:
-PLUG THE DEVICE INTO ANY POWER OUTLET WITH TYPE-C CONNECTOR

STEP 2 OPEN THE WEBSITE:
-ACCESS THE WEBSITE AND ENTER SUPERADMIN ACCOUNT

STEP 3 ADD TEACHER/ADMIN ACCOUNTS:
-ADD SOME TEST/DUMMY ACCOUNTS TO SIMULATE ATTENDANCE 
-CHECK THE EMAIL TO SEE ACCOUNT CREDENTIALS
-LOGOUT

STEP 4 REGISTER FINGERPRINT FOR TEACHER:
-OPEN UP THE DEVICE AND CHOOSE "ENROLL" AND CHOOSE TEACHER
-ENTER YOUR ENROLLMENT CODE
-SCAN YOUR FINGERPRINT TWICE TO REGISTER

STEP 5 ACCESS THE WEBSITE:
-LOGIN TEACHER ACCOUNT
-CLICK MANAGE SECTIONS
-UPLOAD FILE UNDER "CLASS LIST" FOLDER
-CLICK UPLOAD

STEP 6 REGISTER FINGERPRINT FOR STUDENT:
-OPEN THE DEVICE AND CHOOSE "ENROLL" AND CHOOSE STUDENT
-ENTER STUDENT ID
-SCAN YOUR FINGERPRINT TWICE TO REGISTER

STEP 7 ADD SCHEDULE:
-LOGIN THE TEACHER ACCOUNT
-CLICK MANAGE SCHEDULE
-FILL THE DETAILS (SET START TIME TO 1 MINUTE AWAY FROM CURRENT TIME TO TEST)

STEP 8 SIMULATE ATTENDANCE:
-OPEN THE DEVICE AND CHOOSE "SCAN"
-FOLLOW THE INSTRUCTIONS ON THE LED

HOW TO SETUP WIFI ON THE DEVICE:

STEP 1 LOCATE: 
-OPEN THE FINGERPRINT SCANNER FOLDER UNDER attendance_system/FINGERPRINT SCANNER
STEP 2 OPEN ARDUINO FILE:
-CLICK THE "NEW FOLDER" AND OPEN "NEW.ino"
STEP 3 CHANGE WIFI PASSWORD:
-MODIFY THE LINE 18 AND LINE 19 CODE
-CHANGE THE SSID AND PASSWORD INSIDE THE "" AND PUT YOUR WIFI/INTERNET DETAILS 
STEP 4 UPLOAD:
-HOLD DOWN THE BOOT BUTTON ON THE DEVICE AND CLICK UPLOAD TIL THE CONSOLE SHOWS UP.
